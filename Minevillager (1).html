Welcome, Minevillian! 💎

This guide compiles essential tips and commands for navigating the Mineville server, covering economy, ranks, city management, and custom mechanics. Use the color-coded sections below to quickly find what you need!

🛠️ Utility & General Mechanics (Blue)

Item Collection Mechanics

Items collect where the 1st block you break/drop falls. For large farm design, this is critical for efficiency. Video Link

Entity Clear (Despawn)

Mobs (if not named) and items despawn every 15 minutes. This includes boats and minecarts. Entity clear countdown appears in chat.

Minecart Escape (No Teleport)

When exiting normally, you are teleported back to your entry point. To exit without this teleport, use an enderpearl.

Core Mechanics

  • Mob griefing is off.
  • Villagers won't eat to breed (only find/turn existing ones).
  • Creepers/explosions do no block damage.

Merge Tools (Stack Uses)

Combine multiple limited-use items (Hoes, Axes, Wands, TrenchPiks) into one item with more uses using: /mergetools. Video

Automatic Farms

Redstone is disabled and pistons do not push crops/items. The best fully automatic farm is **Cactus**. Pumpkins and Melons sell for more but **cannot be fully automated** (they require a Sell Wand).

🚀 Teleportation & Movement (Blue)

Teleport to Player

Use /tpa PlayerName to request a teleport to another player. They must accept with /tpaccept.

Teleport Player to You

Use /tpahere PlayerName to request a player teleport to your location. They must accept with /tpaccept.

Globe Teleport Menu

Access the main travel menu via your Globe item (inventory) and select the Teleport option to travel to warps, cities, and dimensions.

⌨️ Command Essentials (Blue)

Demigod Utility Commands

Champion+ gets /fix (item in hand), while **Demigod** rank gains exclusive access to /fix all (repairs all inventory items) and /heal.

Champion Utility Commands

The **Champion** rank (and higher) unlocks crucial commands like /fix (repair item in hand), /feed (instantly restore hunger), and the ability to use utility blocks (/anvil, /smithingtable, etc.).

Paladin Utility & Economy Commands

The **Paladin** rank (and higher) gains access to /disposal, /hat, /sellall, and the ability to use the **Sell All** button in /shop.

Check Rank/Fly Time

Use the command /rank to view the time remaining on your purchased ranks. Fly time is typically shown in the **in-game scoreboard**.

Daily Starter Kit

Claim basic F2P supplies every 24 hours using the command: /kit starter.

🌾 Farming & Optimization (Neon Green)

Hoppers Limit

You can only place a maximum of 100 hoppers per city. Plan your collection systems wisely!

Item Frame Limit

There is a limit of 30 item frames per chunk. This limit prevents lag and entity overload in large builds.

Most Profitable Crops

The best crops for profit are Cactus (easy to fully automate) and Nether Wart (high selling price but requires manual harvest/sell wand).

AFK Strategy (Gems)

To earn Gems (for /gemshop ranks/perks), use /afk (1 Gem / 5 mins). If you use an AFK pool, remember the 15-minute entity clear.

AFK Pond Event Usage

You **cannot** participate in the Pond event while in the **AFK Room** using the /afk command. You **can** use the Pond event if you are AFK on your own city claim.

🏠 City & Claim Management (Neon Green)

Claims Expiry Warning

If you don't play for 21 days, all your claims will unclaim, and you lose your city. Log on periodically to prevent griefing.

Chunk Border Calculation (16x16)

To manually find a chunk border (where a claim starts/ends): Check your X, Y, Z coordinates. If X or Z is exactly divisible by 16 (e.g., 16, 32, 48...), you are on a chunk border. Alternatively, use Globe -> City to see the current chunk coordinates.

🛑 DO NOT DELETE CITY

Deleting your city means you lose ALL your extra claims (purchased and earned). If moving, set at least one new claim BEFORE deleting the old ones.

Removing Annoying Visitors

Use this strict two-step process to ensure they can't return: 1. /city ban PlayerName (prevents re-entry) then 2. /city kick PlayerName (removes them immediately).

💰 Shops & Economy (Gold)

📦 QuickShop Command Reference (/qs)

/qs create

Creates a new shop from the targeted chest. This is the first step to setting up a shop!

/qs remove

Removes the shop you are currently looking at.

/qs sell

Changes an existing shop to **SELL** mode, allowing other players to buy from you.

/qs buy

Changes an existing shop to **BUY** mode, allowing you to buy items from other players.

/qs price [new price]

Changes the buy or sell price of the shop you are looking at.

/qs amount [new amount]

Allows you to set the amount of items for a transaction (useful when chat input is difficult).

/qs staff add [username]

Adds a player (co-op/staff) to your shop, allowing them to access the inventory of the chest shop.

/qs name [new name]

Changes the name/display text of the specific shop you are looking at.

/qs item

Changes the display item of the shop to the item you are currently holding.

/qs suggestprice

Suggests a recommended price for the shop you are looking at, based on prices in other player shops.

/qs find [item name]

Locates the nearest shop selling/buying a specific type of item.

/qs fetchmessage

Displays unread messages related to your QuickShops (e.g., successful transactions).

/qs about

Shows general information about the QuickShop plugin currently running on the server.

/qs help

Shows the comprehensive QuickShop help menu in-game.

Max Shops & Rank Perks

If you obtain a rank, set your maximum number of shops immediately. You keep the higher shop count even if the rank expires! (F2P: 10 shops, Hero: 40, Paladin: 50, Champion: 75, **Demigod: 100**).

Sellable Items in Shops

You can sell Ender Chests in your chest shops, which is a popular item for high-end players.

Beginner Money Method

Best way: Globe - Economy - Manage Jobs. Get the Digger job and mine soul sand/soil. Paladin+ can join 2 jobs, Demigod+ can join 3. Jobs Video

Set a Shop Quickly

Hold item, look at a chest, and type: /chestshop. No sign needed. F2P: 10 shops. Demi: 100 shops.

Jobs & Custom Items

Limited use items (Harvester Hoe, Trench Axe) and Custom Enchants (like Timber) DO NOT give you money or XP in your job. Manual harvesting is required for job pay.

📜 Auctions, Enchants & Chat (Bright Orange)

Item Renaming & Custom Formatting (/rename)

The /rename command is unlocked by the **Demigod** rank (and higher). All color and formatting codes **MUST** be prefixed with &.

**Example Command:** /rename &6&L MARV MORPHBLADE
**Glitch Text Example:** /rename &b&k GLITCH &r&5 MESSAGE &b&k GLITCH

Formatting Codes:
  • &L: Large Letters (Bold)
  • &O: Italics
  • &K: Symbols (Glitch)
  • &J: Symbols (Glitch)
  • &R: Reset to Regular
Color Codes:
  • &A: Lime
  • &B: Teal
  • &C: Pink
  • &D: Magenta
  • &E: Yellow
  • &F: White
  • &1: Navy
  • &2: Green
  • &3: Cyan
  • &4: Red
  • &5: Purple
  • &6: Gold
  • &7: Grey
  • &8: Dark Grey
  • &9: Violet
  • &0: Black

Get Shards for Custom Enchants

You can buy Shards (used to get Custom Enchants) from the Custom Enchant shop using your **XP levels**.

AH Restrictions & Issues

The bid, gem functions, and auction timer DO NOT work in the Auction House. Also, you cannot sell: **Ender Chests, Shulker Boxes, Chests, Barrels, or Name Tags** in the AH.

Chat Colors & Glitches

Use color codes (like &e for yellow) and the obfuscated glitch code (Ex: &e&kABC) to make messages stand out. Note that the glitch codes are sometimes incomplete.

Item Linking in Chat

Hold the item you want to advertise and simply type [i] in the chat window to create a clickable item link for viewers.

Custom Enchants Doc

Refer to BabyShark's continually updated Google Doc for a list of CEs: CE Google Doc. Use /customenchants for in-game list.

👾 Spawners & Mobs (Light Indigo)

Spawner Mobs are Passive

Mobs from spawners are "dumb" and won't attack you. No need for a fancy grinder, just attack what spawns.

Mining Spawners (Ranked Perk)

You need a Hero Rank (or higher) to mine spawners using a Silk Touch pickaxe. F2P players must ask a ranked player for help.

🌎 Events & Dimensions (Bright Cyan)

Random Giveaways

Keep an eye on the in-game chat for **random staff-hosted giveaways** throughout the day. Make sure to attend them to potentially win great prizes!

Pond Event (/warp pond)

When fishing, catch the words jumping out of the pond, don't fish normally. Happens every 4 hours at **1:00, 5:00, 9:00, 13:00, 17:00, 21:00 EST/EDT.**

Daily Server Updates (Kick Warning)

Earth servers update daily, usually between 5:00-6:00 AM EST/EDT. You are kicked if on that server. TP to another location/dimension to avoid the kick.

OneBlock Chests (Ranked Perk)

You need a Rank to open the special phase chests and see/get the goodies inside.
